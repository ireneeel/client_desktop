UMSI Food Pantry

Welcome to the UMSI Food Pantry

The UMSI Food Pantry is dedicated to supporting students, faculty, and staff in need by providing access to nutritious and non-perishable food items. We understand the challenges that come with managing finances while balancing academic responsibilities, and we're here to help. Our mission is to ensure that no one in our community goes hungry.

How It Works

Using the UMSI Food Pantry is simple and confidential. Here's how you can access our services:

  • Visit our pantry during open hours or request an appointment for a more private visit.
  • Select from a variety of available food items, including canned goods, fresh produce, and personal care products.
  • All visits are confidential, and no personal information will be shared outside the pantry team.

Donation Opportunities

We rely on donations to keep our pantry stocked and running. If you'd like to support the UMSI Food Pantry, here are some ways to contribute:

  • Donate non-perishable food items such as canned vegetables, pasta, and grains.
  • Contribute personal care products like soap, shampoo, and toothpaste.
  • Monetary donations are always welcome and help us purchase fresh produce and other essentials.
