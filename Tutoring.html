Tutoring

Welcome to UMSI Tutoring

At UMSI, we offer tutoring services to ensure students can achieve their academic goals. Our tutors are knowledgeable, patient, and ready to assist you in a wide range of subjects. Whether you need help with understanding a particular concept, preparing for an exam, or completing an assignment, we are here to help.

Available Tutoring Services

We offer tutoring in the following areas:

  • Data Science & Programming: Guidance on coding assignments, data analysis, and tools like Python, R, and SQL.
  • User Experience Design: Assistance with design projects, prototyping, and user-centered research methods.
  • Information Science & Management: Support in information systems, project management, and data handling.

How to Access Tutoring Services

To make the most of UMSI's tutoring services, you can either drop by during open tutoring hours or schedule a one-on-one session. Here's how to get started:

  • Visit the Tutoring Center located in Room 204 of the UMSI building.
  • Check the tutoring schedule online and select a time that fits your needs.
  • For specialized assistance, schedule a one-on-one session through the tutoring portal.

Meet Our Tutors

Our tutors are graduate students and professionals with expertise in their respective fields. Check their profiles to find the best fit for your needs.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need help scheduling a session, contact the tutoring center at tutoring@umsi.edu. We look forward to assisting you!
